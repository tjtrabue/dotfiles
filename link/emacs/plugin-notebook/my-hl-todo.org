Highlight TODO, and keywords such as FIXME, DEBUG, GOTCHA, and STUB in files.

The package provides two minor modes: =hl-todo-mode= and =global-hl-minor-mode=.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
  (use-package hl-todo
    :hook
    (prog-mode . hl-todo-mode))
#+end_src
