=beacon= is a global minor-mode for briefly highlighting your cursor as it jumps
around a window. Very pretty and very helpful.

Needed for performance:
#+begin_src emacs-lisp :tangle yes
;; -*- lexical-binding: t; -*-

#+end_src

#+begin_src emacs-lisp :tangle yes
  (use-package beacon
    :config
    (beacon-mode 1))
#+end_src
